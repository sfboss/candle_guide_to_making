Taper Candles¶

Methods¶

  • Dipping or molding

Steps (Dipping)¶

  1. Melt beeswax; maintain stable temp
  2. Dip pairs repeatedly, cooling between dips

Warning

Keep water bath for control; prevent flare-ups.
